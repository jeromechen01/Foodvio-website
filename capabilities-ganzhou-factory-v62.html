01 / Why Ganzhou

为什么把旗舰工厂建在赣州? Why we built the flagship in Ganzhou.

赣南红土地是中国地理标志产品**赣南螺丝椒**的原产地——这颗辣椒撑起了花花酱料和发酵小菜的灵魂。把工厂建在原料的旁边,不只是物流考虑,更是品质考虑:原料冷链直采,鲜度不流失。 The red soil of Gannan is the home of Geographical-Indication-protected Gannan Luosijiao chili — the soul of Foodvio's sauce and fermented side products. Building a factory next to the source isn't just logistics — it's quality. Cold-chain direct sourcing, freshness preserved.

赣州 6.66 万㎡ 综合工厂,是我们 8 大类 SC 食品生产许可的母厂——意味着花花 100+ SKU 中的大部分,都在这里被打样、量产、装箱。 Our 66,600㎡ Ganzhou integrated facility serves as the mother plant for all 8 SC food production licenses — meaning the majority of Foodvio's 100+ SKUs are prototyped, mass-produced, and packed here.
