01 / The Network

一体化供应链An integrated supply network.

很多西餐品牌的痛点是供应链碎片化:原料外采、生产外包、物流外包——质量没法控,成本没法降,出问题没法追溯。我们走了**完全相反的路**:从产地到客户端,所有环节自营或长期独家合作。 Most Western food brands suffer from fragmented supply chains: outsourced sourcing, outsourced production, outsourced logistics. Quality slips, costs balloon, traceability vanishes. We took **the exact opposite path**: from origin to client, every link is owned or long-term exclusive.

结果就是:**3,000+ 客户**,不需要担心断货 / 串货 / 来源不明。 Result: **3,000+ partners** never worry about stockouts, channel conflict, or unknown sourcing.
