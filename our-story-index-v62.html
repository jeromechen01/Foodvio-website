03 / Our Journey

花花食界大事记 The Foodvio journey

从 2021 到今天 · 一年一个里程碑 From 2021 to today · one milestone a year

2021Aug.

花花食界创立Foodvio founded

北京望京 SOHO 起步,瞄准"源自中国的专业西餐食品"赛道。Founded at Wangjing SOHO, Beijing — targeting the "professional Western cuisine from China" lane.

2022

首批 50 款西式产品通过 SC 认证First 50 Western SKUs SC-certified

建立 6 大产品线雏形,服务首批 100+ 餐饮客户。Six product lines take shape, serving first 100+ F&B partners.

2023

河北张家口工厂获 FDA 注册Zhangjiakou facility FDA-registered

烘焙工厂打通海外通路,为出海打下合规基础。Bakery facility opens overseas channels, building export-ready compliance.

2024

江西赣州工厂扩建至 6.66 万㎡Ganzhou facility expanded to 66,600㎡

赣南螺丝椒供应链一体化,8 大类 SC 资质布局完成。Gannan chili supply chain integrated, 8 SC categories in place.

2025

FOB 首批出货东南亚First FOB shipment to Southeast Asia

从天津、青岛、上海、深圳 4 大港口起运,服务海外客户。Departing from Tianjin, Qingdao, Shanghai, Shenzhen — overseas customers go live.

2026

双品牌矩阵正式发布Dual-brand matrix launched

花花食界(B 端 80%)+ 华仔爸爸 ChinnPaPa(C 端 20%),双品牌完整矩阵成型。Foodvio (B2B · 80%) + ChinnPaPa (Consumer · 20%) — dual-brand matrix formally announced.
