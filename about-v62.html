01 / Who We Are

我们是谁 Who We Are

北京花花食界食品科技有限公司创立于 2021 年 8 月,总部位于北京市朝阳区零秒空间。我们是国内领先的专业西餐食品供应链品牌,以"还原世界味道,享受美食乐趣"为产品理念。 Beijing Foodvio Food Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in August 2021, headquartered at LIMO space in Chaoyang, Beijing. We are a leading professional Western cuisine food supply chain brand from China, dedicated to restoring world flavors and bringing the joy of good food.

创始团队由原国内食品上市公司总裁及核心成员组成,在食品行业拥有卓越的声誉和丰富的经验。我们高度重视产品研发与原料还原度,自成立以来共投入研发费用数千万元,拟获批食品类目专利十余项。 The founding team consists of the former president and core members of a publicly-listed Chinese food company, bringing distinguished reputation and rich industry experience. We highly value R&D and authenticity restoration, having invested tens of millions of RMB in R&D since founding, with over 10 food-category patents pending.

我们覆盖近 10 万平方米自建及托管生产空间(江西赣州 · 河北张家口 · 北京怀柔三大基地),100+ 款 SKU,服务 3,000+ 家客户,销售网络覆盖全国 10 余个省市。 We operate nearly 100,000 m² of self-built and managed production facilities across three bases (Ganzhou · Zhangjiakou · Huairou Beijing), with 100+ SKUs, serving 3,000+ clients across 10+ provinces and cities.
