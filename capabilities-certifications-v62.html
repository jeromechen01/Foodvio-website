01 / Why we welcome the audit

我们欢迎你审核We welcome the audit.

食品行业有太多"自我宣称":自称健康、自称无添加、自称源头直采。**我们的态度不一样**:我们不只列认证,我们公布证书号、有效期、发证机构,欢迎你随时核验或派第三方来工厂审核。 The food industry has too many "self-claims" — self-proclaimed healthy, self-proclaimed clean-label, self-proclaimed origin-sourced. **Our stance is different**: we don't just list certs — we publish numbers, expiry dates, issuing bodies. Verify any time. Send your auditor to our plant.

这不是"我们值得信任",这是"你不需要信任我们,你可以验证"。 This isn't "trust us." This is "you don't have to trust us — you can verify."

事实是:我们的产品已远销美国、澳大利亚、加拿大等 10 多个国家和地区 —— 国际买家用订单替我们的认证盖章。 The proof: our products ship to 10+ countries including the US, Australia, and Canada. International buyers stamp our certifications with purchase orders.

04 / Third-party Testing

第三方检测体系Third-party testing.

每批次,每检测Every batch, every test.

SGS / Bureau Veritas 等国际第三方机构合作。每个出厂批次随机抽检以下项目: Partner with SGS / Bureau Veritas. Each outgoing batch randomly tested for:

  • · 微生物指标(菌落总数 / 大肠菌群 / 致病菌)Microbiological (total count / coliform / pathogens)
  • · 重金属残留(铅 / 镉 / 汞 / 砷)Heavy metals (Pb / Cd / Hg / As)
  • · 农药残留(对原料蔬菜 / 香料)Pesticide residue (vegetables / spices)
  • · 食品添加剂限量Food additive limits
  • · 过敏原管控(尤其烘焙线)Allergen control (esp. bakery)

📋 客户索取 → 提供该批次检测报告 📋 Client request → batch test report provided
