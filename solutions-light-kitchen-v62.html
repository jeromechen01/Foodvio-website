01 / Customer Pains

轻后厨的
6 大客户痛点 6 pain points
of light kitchens.

不论你做的是咖啡、亲子餐饮还是文旅轻餐 —— 都绕不开这六件事。 Whether you serve coffee, family meals, or museum-side menus — these six issues are inescapable.

📐

后厨面积有限Limited kitchen footprint

专业披萨炉、意面工作台都摆不下,设备投入再高也施展不开。No room for specialty ovens or pasta stations — equipment investment doesn't translate into output.

👨‍🍳

厨师少 · 用工成本高Lean staff, high labor cost

西餐主厨月薪高,小品牌请不起,大品牌也很难长期留人。Western chefs cost premium wages — small brands can't afford them, large ones struggle to retain.

⏱️

高峰期出餐压力大Peak-hour service strain

晚高峰、周末、节假日,客流一来就忙不过来,出餐不稳定。Evenings, weekends, holidays — when traffic spikes, output stability collapses.

📋

餐食品类不足Narrow menu, missed revenue

菜单单薄,客单价提不上来,缺少营收增长点。Thin menu means low ticket size — no engine for revenue growth.

🔄

菜单固化 · 上新难Menu fatigue, slow turnover

季度上新跟不上,菜单老旧,熟客回头率下滑。Quarterly launches stall — dated menus erode repeat visits.

📦

食材损耗高 · 库存难管High waste, hard inventory

原材料杂、保质期短,采购预估失准就直接砸成本。Mixed raw materials, short shelf life — every misjudged purchase becomes pure loss.

重点案例
某顶级主题乐园连锁 Featured case
A top-tier theme park chain.

从单店合作,到跨园区体系化对接 —— 一个真实的轻后厨升级路径。 From single-park trial to chain-wide rollout — a real light-kitchen upgrade path.

⊙ 客户介绍Customer profile

国内某头部主题乐园集团旗下连锁品牌,以科幻和互动体验为特色,园区遍布全国,常年承载大量亲子及游客消费场景。 A flagship chain under a major Chinese theme-park group, known for sci-fi and interactive entertainment, with parks across the country serving high volumes of family and traveler traffic year-round.

⊙ 核心痛点Pain points
  • · 晚高峰游客集中,出餐压力大Evening peak — service strain
  • · 披萨、意面、炸食等小吃产品质不稳定Pizza / pasta / fried snacks — quality inconsistent
  • · 后厨团队庞大,管理难,人员效率不高Large kitchen team, hard to manage, low efficiency
  • · 食材浪费严重,损耗高High food waste
  • · 菜品单一,新品推出难Narrow menu, slow innovation
  • · 各地餐厅出品不统一,缺少标准化形象Inconsistent output across locations
⊙ 解决方案Solution
  • · 全程可追溯高品质产品支持Fully traceable, premium-grade supply
  • · 每月新品上线,按季度迭代Monthly new SKUs, quarterly rotations
  • · 成品菜方案,提升出餐效率Ready-to-serve dish design, faster service
  • · 亲子同乐及派对套餐设计Family & party set menu design
  • · 菜单设计 / 灯箱设计 / 展示设计支持Menu, lightbox, and display design support
  • · 助力形成统一标准化西式餐品形象Build a chain-wide standardized Western-cuisine identity
⊙ 合作成果Outcome

已与多个园区门店达成合作,并已与该集团总部对接 —— 就体系化战略合作达成初步共识,未来将为整个连锁体系提供西式餐饮餐品服务支持。 Partnerships established at multiple park locations, with chain-wide strategic alignment underway at the group's HQ — laying the path to support the entire chain's Western-cuisine F&B service.
