01 / Opening

中国厨房里的两块黑钻石 Two black diamonds in the Chinese kitchen.

Two black diamonds in the Chinese kitchen. 中国厨房里的两块黑钻石

云南黑松露,年产 100-300 吨,占全国 70%、全球 80%。这个数字意味着——法国年产量是云南的十分之一。每公斤云南黑松露批发价 ¥586,法国黑松露 ¥5,157,价差 8-9 倍。同一种风味、同一个学名、巨大的价格鸿沟。

Yunnan produces 100-300 tonnes of black truffle annually — 70% of China's total, 80% of the world's. France produces a tenth of what Yunnan does. Yunnan wholesale: ¥586/kg. French wholesale: ¥5,157/kg. An 8 to 9-fold price gap. Same flavor, same scientific name, vast price chasm.

再往南一点,昭通、玉溪、保山,是云南迷迭香的种植带。中国 70% 的迷迭香原料来自这里——但迷迭香的故事更复杂:云南负责种植和初加工,真正的精深加工和出口枢纽,在海南自贸港。这是"云南种植 + 海南加工出口"的天然产业分工。

Further south — Zhaotong, Yuxi, Baoshan — runs Yunnan's rosemary belt. 70% of China's rosemary raw material comes from this strip. But rosemary has a more complex story. Yunnan handles cultivation and primary processing; the deep processing and export hub sits in Hainan's free trade port. A natural division of labor: Yunnan grows, Hainan refines.

黑松露是花花食界从"对标进口的国产替代"升级为"中国独占资源的全球叙事"的关键支点。迷迭香是花花从中国市场走向全球贸易的实战载体。

Black truffle is our pivot — from "China replacement of imports" to "Chinese-only resource on the global stage." Rosemary is our practical vehicle for moving from the China market into global trade.

“罗勒和迷迭香的故事,
是花花在中国做"国产替代"。
黑松露的故事,
是花花向全球讲一个欧洲也无法复制的中国故事。”
“Basil and rosemary tell a story of replacing imports inside China.
Black truffle tells the world a Chinese story that Europe cannot copy.”
—— 花花食界研发主厨 — Foodvio R&D Chef
02 / Origin

一座山脉,两条产业线 One mountain range, two product lines.

One mountain range, two product lines. 一座山脉,两条产业线

云南楚雄、永仁、丽江——黑松露的核心产区。海拔 1,800-2,800 米的横断山脉、典型的 "高原低纬度" 气候、橡树和栎树形成的天然共生森林——这些条件让云南成为全球最大的黑松露产地。这不是任何商业行为的结果,而是地质和气候的礼物。

Chuxiong, Yongren, Lijiang — Yunnan's truffle core. Hengduan Mountains at 1,800-2,800m, the rare "high-altitude low-latitude" climate, natural symbiosis with oak forests. Together, the conditions that made Yunnan the world's largest truffle producer. Not a commercial achievement — a geological and climatic gift.

牟定、昭通、玉溪——迷迭香的种植带。云南山地的酸性红壤、海拔差产生的层次温度、充足光照——这是迷迭香生长的理想区。但云南的产业链止步于初加工,真正的精油提取、活性物质分离、欧美出口认证,在海南自贸港完成——这一段产业分工,是中国日报 2022 年公开报道过的事实。

Mouding, Zhaotong, Yuxi — Yunnan's rosemary belt. Acidic red soil, altitude-driven temperature gradients, abundant sunlight — ideal growing conditions. But the value chain stops at primary processing in Yunnan. Essential oil extraction, active compound isolation, EU/US export certification — all of that happens in Hainan's free trade port. A division of labor reported by China Daily in 2022.

云南给我们的,不只是两种原料,是两种叙事——"中国独有"和"中国 + 全球加工链路"。

Yunnan gives us more than two ingredients. It gives us two narratives — "only in China," and "China plus a global processing route."

03 / Why we chose it

为什么花花把云南放在最后? Why we saved Yunnan for last.

Three reasons. From a chef's notebook. 三个理由 · 来自主厨笔记
01

全球叙事 —— 黑松露重新定义品牌天花板 Global narrative — black truffle resets our brand ceiling

在花花做赣南辣椒、新疆番茄、内蒙牛羊肉的时候,叙事都是"中国能做出对标进口的好东西"。但云南黑松露不一样——它本身就是世界第一,法国都做不到这个量级。当我们对全球客户讲"花花用的黑松露,世界上 80% 都来自我们的合作产区",这一刻,品牌天花板就被重新定义了。

Building Gannan chili, Xinjiang tomato, and Inner Mongolian beef, the narrative was always "China can match imports." Yunnan truffle changes the frame. It is the world leader — France can't approach the volume. When we tell global buyers "80% of the world's black truffle comes from our partner zones," the brand ceiling resets in that sentence.

02

价格优势 —— 让进口主厨第一次愿意用国产 Price gap — for the first time, imported-chef territory uses Chinese ingredient

8-9 倍的价差不是宣传话术——这是写在批发市场报价单上的事实。一道意式黑松露烩饭,用法国黑松露成本 ¥80,用云南黑松露成本 ¥10。当成本压到这个水平,不仅本土西餐厅愿意用,我们做出口时,东南亚和中东的高端餐饮也开始问"你们的黑松露是不是来自云南?"——这个问题在五年前是不存在的。

An 8-9x price gap isn't a marketing line — it's the wholesale market quote. A truffle risotto plate costs ¥80 with French truffle, ¥10 with Yunnan. At that delta, not only do domestic Western restaurants switch, but when we export, premium operators in Southeast Asia and the Middle East ask "is your truffle from Yunnan?" — a question no one was asking five years ago.

03

云南-海南枢纽 —— 全球化路径已经存在 Yunnan-Hainan hub — the global route already exists

迷迭香的产业链早就分工好了:云南种植、海南加工、欧美出口。我们做的事不是开创新路径,而是把这条已经存在的产业链整合进花花的产品矩阵。这意味着——花花的迷迭香系列产品,从第一天起就具备出海能力。我们不需要再花十年建一条出口链路。

The rosemary value chain is already split: Yunnan grows, Hainan processes, exports flow to Europe and the US. We don't have to build new infrastructure — we plug into what already exists. From day one, our rosemary products are export-capable. We don't need to spend a decade building outbound logistics.
