03 / Our Promise

溯源承诺 Our sourcing promise.

对每一颗原料,我们都做到这三件事。 For every ingredient, we promise three things.

🔬

第三方独立检测Third-party verification

每一批次原料,均经过 SGS 或 BV 等独立第三方检测机构进行品质与安全检验,报告随产品同步交付。Every batch is tested by independent SGS or BV facilities for quality and safety. Reports delivered alongside product.

📋

批次全链路可追溯Full-chain batch traceability

从原料田间到成品出厂,每一批次都有完整的追溯记录。任何一袋花花产品,都能查到它来自哪一片土地。From field to finished product, every batch carries a complete traceability log. Any Foodvio package — we can tell you what soil it came from.

🚜

每年 2 次产地访查Twice-yearly origin audit

我们的供应链团队每年至少 2 次实地访查每个核心产地,与农户、合作社建立直接联系,长期合作并不通过中间商。Our supply chain team visits each core origin at least twice per year — building direct, long-term partnerships without middlemen.
