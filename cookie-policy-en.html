01 · What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files sent by a web server to your browser when you visit a site, and stored on your device (computer, phone, tablet). They contain information about your interactions with the site, enabling the site to remember your preferences or actions.

This Site also uses similar technologies including local storage (localStorage / sessionStorage). This Policy applies to those technologies as well.

04 · Cookies and Personal Information

Essential cookies generally do not directly collect personally identifying information, except:

  • Inquiry form data (name, email, phone) is collected through a separate channel and stored independently of cookies
  • Aggregated analytics is based on anonymized IP and browser fingerprint and does not identify you directly

For details on personal information processing, see our Privacy Policy.

05 · "Do Not Track" Signals

Some browsers support Do Not Track (DNT). Industry has not standardized DNT handling, so this Site does not respond to DNT signals. However, our practices already align with DNT's spirit:

  • No cross-site tracking
  • No advertising cookies
  • No sharing of browsing behavior with third parties

  • Re-prompting via the Cookie preference modal where new consent is required
