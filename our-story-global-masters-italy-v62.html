01 / Opening

2018 年的春天,我抵达那不勒斯 I arrived in Naples in the spring of 2018.

From a Beijing kitchen to a wood-fired oven in Naples. 从北京的厨房到那不勒斯的石窑

2018 年春天,我推开 Naples 老城区一家披萨店的木门时,Antonio 大师正在揉面。他扫了我一眼,说了句:"你不是意大利人,所以你要花更长时间。" 然后递给我一袋面粉。这是我意大利学艺的第一天。

Spring 2018. I pushed open the wooden door of a small pizzeria in old Naples. Master Antonio was kneading dough. He glanced at me and said: "You're not Italian. It will take you longer." Then he handed me a sack of flour. That was day one of my apprenticeship.

三年后,我带着一本翻烂了的笔记本回到北京。本子里记着 14 小时慢炖肉酱的火候曲线,记着石窑温度从 350°C 降到 280°C 的判断方法,记着帕尔玛奶酪 18 个月与 24 个月的味觉差异。这些笔记,后来成了花花食界 18 款意式产品的灵魂。

Three years later, I came back to Beijing with a worn notebook. In it: temperature curves for 14-hour slow-cooked Bolognese; how to read a wood-fired oven dropping from 350°C to 280°C; the taste difference between 18-month and 24-month Parmigiano. Those notes became the soul of 18 Italian products at Foodvio.

本期 Master Story,我带你回到那段意式学艺旅程。

In this Master Story, I'll take you back to that journey.

plus patience with time."

03 / Three lessons

带回中国的三个核心技艺 Three core lessons brought home.

From a chef's notebook. 主厨笔记
01

石窑发酵 —— 真正的披萨饼底始于面团 Wood-fired fermentation — the real pizza begins with the dough

那不勒斯人对面团的执着到了苛刻的程度。他们坚信:配方再好,如果发酵不对,披萨就是失败的。我学到的关键是 24 小时冷藏低温发酵 + 常温 4 小时醒发的双段发酵法,以及面团下锅时的湿度控制(58%-62%)。这些细节后来全部转化成了我们工厂的标准化作业流程。

Neapolitans treat dough with religious seriousness. Their conviction: no matter how good your recipe, bad fermentation kills the pizza. The key technique I learned: 24-hour cold ferment, then 4-hour warm rise — a two-stage method, with hydration controlled between 58%-62% before the oven. Those exact specifications now sit inside our factory SOPs.

02

14 小时慢炖 —— 时间是最贵的调味料 14 hours of slow simmer — time is the most expensive seasoning

Nonna Maria 的肉酱秘诀,听上去简单到让人怀疑:小火、加红酒、加牛奶、慢炖 14 小时。但当你看着她从早晨 6 点开始,每隔 30 分钟搅一次锅,直到下午 8 点,你才理解什么叫"功夫菜"。这种慢炖的火候控制,我们后来用工业化设备复刻了出来 —— 这是"古法肉酱意面"的基础。

Nonna Maria's Bolognese sounded almost too simple: low heat, red wine, milk, 14 hours of simmer. But watching her start at 6 AM and stir every 30 minutes until 8 PM — you understand what slow cooking actually means. We later reverse-engineered that heat profile into industrial equipment. That's the backbone of our "Classic Bolognese Pasta."

03

帕尔玛干酪 —— 18 个月与 24 个月的差异决定一切 Parmigiano — the difference between 18 months and 24 months decides everything

米兰那家米其林餐厅,他们对帕尔玛奶酪有近乎偏执的标准:18 个月用于撒粉,24 个月用于切片直接入口,30 个月以上用于做酱。这种细分让我意识到原料分级的力量。我们今天的"奶油蘑菇酱""牛肝菌酱"严格按熟成期分级使用,正是这一课的延续。

That Milan kitchen ran an obsessive Parmigiano protocol: 18-month for grating, 24-month sliced for direct eating, 30+ months reserved for sauces. The discipline of ingredient tiering became fundamental to me. Our Cream of Mushroom and Porcini sauces today still grade by aging — a direct legacy of that lesson.
