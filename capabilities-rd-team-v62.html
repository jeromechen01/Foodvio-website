01 / The Team

这是花花最贵的资产The most valuable asset Foodvio owns.

工厂可以建,设备可以买,认证可以申请——但**研发主厨,无法靠钱买,只能靠时间培养**。我们的研发团队由资深主厨组成,每一位都在海外学艺多年,然后回到北京怀柔基地驻扎,负责把世界风味重构给中国客户。 Factories can be built, equipment can be bought, certifications can be applied for. But **R&D chefs cannot be bought — only built over time**. Our team is made up of seasoned chefs, each apprenticed overseas for years, now stationed at our Huairou R&D base, rebuilding world flavors for Chinese clients.

覆盖意大利 / 西班牙 / 韩国 / 日本 / 东南亚 / 墨西哥 6 国——这是花花产品矩阵的真正来源。 Spanning Italy / Spain / Korea / Japan / SEA / Mexico — this is the true source of Foodvio's product matrix.
